Frequently Asked Questions

01

How do I know if I need a hearing test?

Click to reveal answer

Common signs include asking people to repeat themselves, difficulty hearing in noisy environments, turning up the TV volume higher than others prefer, or feeling that people are mumbling.

Book Assessment
02

What happens during a hearing assessment?

Click to reveal answer

A comprehensive assessment includes case history discussion, ear examination, pure-tone audiometry testing, speech discrimination testing, and detailed results explanation. Takes 60-90 minutes.

Learn More
03

How long do hearing aids last?

Click to reveal answer

With proper care and maintenance, hearing aids typically last 4-7 years. Regular professional cleaning and annual check-ups can extend their lifespan and ensure optimal performance.

Maintenance Info
04

Can tinnitus be treated?

Click to reveal answer

While there's no cure, tinnitus can be effectively managed through sound therapy, hearing aids with masking features, counselling, and lifestyle modifications. Our programs help reduce its impact significantly.

Tinnitus Help
05

Do you offer workplace hearing testing?

Click to reveal answer

Yes! We provide workplace hearing screening for WHS compliance. Employees can visit our clinic for comprehensive audiometric assessments with detailed reports for employer records.
